SECURISYNC


SecuriSync helps you secure and manage your critical business data. SecuriSync enhances traditional file sync and share by adding real-time backup, advanced anti-malware/antivirus and restore capabilities.

It integrates with Exchange, Office 365, Outlook, Office and existing file servers and is backed by Woodworks PCS’s 99.999% uptime SLA. It provides your employees with an easy-to-use, secure way to access and collaborate on files from any device.

SecuriSync offers a feature-rich service providing many additional revenue opportunities. It streamlines multiple file management solutions into one, simplifying management and costs. Plus SecuriSync is integrated with BitDefender anti-malware to continuously protect your files.
